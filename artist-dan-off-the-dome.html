Atlanta–based producer Aidan Bonner, known commonly as Dan Off The Dome, creates his sound defined by heavy 808s, moody melodies, and atmospheric textures. His beats have been heard around the country, with his latest release landing on Atlanta artist Lil Tony’s album Tkey Vs. Tony. Next up is Off The Dome, his debut producer tape — a project that fuses music and fashion into one creative movement. As he continues building, expect more collaborations, more sound expansion, and more music straight off the dome
